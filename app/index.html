Hackathon-on-train is once-in-a-lifetime experience for you to share with talented geeks, experienced mentors and business angels from Belarus and Latvia.

Starting at Imaguru Minsk on August 30th, continuing on a train in Riga, finishing after our arrival in Riga and ending at final stage in the center of Riga Startup Community. Hackathon-on-train is organized for the first time in Minsk and Riga.

Imaguru Hackathon-on-train is organized with the support of Investment and Development Agency of Latvia. Imaguru Hackathon-on-train attracts developers, designers and entrepreneurs from Belarus and Latvia, ready to take on-train challenge and disrupt the reality with innovative ideas and prototypes.

FAQ

How do I get my train ticket?

We select teams and provide them with tickets, accommodation and visa support. Just fill in the registration form with your idea and team members and you are half way #hackathonOnTrain

From where is the Hackathon-on-train departing?

The check-in for participants will be organized at Imaguru, we all departure from Minsk Central Station.

What time is the Hackathon-on-train departing?

The check-in for participants will be organized at Imaguru in 18:30, we all departure from Minsk Central Station in 20:30.

How / When do we get to the hackathon from Riga?

If you are selected we will provide you with tickets Riga-Minsk on Aug 27th or Aug 29th. You will learn the local ecosystem, meet new friends and join the hackathon on Aug 30th at Imaguru.

What about the return trip?

If you are selected we will provide you with tickets Minsk-Riga on Sept 1st. You will learn the local ecosystem, and meet new friends.

Do I need any visa?

If you are EU citizen, you need a visa to get to Belarus. We will take care about visas. If you are Belarus citizen, you need a Schengen visa to get to Latvia. If you don't have one we will take care about that.

How about the internet on-train?

Our partners will take care about 3G or 4G Internet connection in train from Minsk to Riga.
